Pixel Art
_________________________________________________________________________________________

This app uses HTML, CSS, and JS.  

HTML - making the individual squares and colours

CSS - styling the rows and text

JS - selecting the colour, and colouring the background of the square with your selected colour

_________________________________________________________________________________________

How to Use:
1. Open index.html
2. Choose colour and click on squares to fill square with selected colour
_________________________________________________________________________________________

Notes:
1. Does not require installation
2. Requires browser
