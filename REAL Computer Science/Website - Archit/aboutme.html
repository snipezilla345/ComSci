My Love for Hockey

My name is Archit Patel and I love hockey. Ever since I was a little kid, my family and I would gather every Saturday to watch the Toronto Maple Leafs play on T.V. My love for hockey grew every Saturday. Because of this, I asked my dad to sign me up for our local hockey league, the Mississauga Hockey Leauge (MHL). I played in that league for 6 years until I switched to ball hockey. Hockey has always been apart of my life in some way and I always felt connected to the sport in some way which is why I love the game so much.
