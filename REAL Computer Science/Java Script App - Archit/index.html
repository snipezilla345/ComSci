Flashlight

The Flashlight + Google Map App is an all in one app with a working flashlight and a map of Brampton highlighting the various attractions around you. Below, you will find a list of steps on how to use the features of the flashlight and map.

  1. Use the "ON" button to turn the flashlight on and the "OFF" button to turn the flashlight off.
  2. To go to the map, click the "Map" button on the top of the screen.
  3. The icons on the map show you the various attractions all around Brampton and near your location (if your geolocation is turned on). You can also use the drop down menu to add your own icons on the map.
  4. Finally, on the top middle of the map you will find a draw feature that allows you to draw lines and various shapes on th map.
